Climate Impact Explorer

DigitalTwin Earth Precursor

Climate Impact Explorer is a demonstration project sponsored by European Space Agency (ESA) in support of the EU Destination Earth (DestinE) initiative, moving towards a predictive decision support capability for European environmental policies.

It is a UK based collaboration between Telespazio, the National Centre for Earth Observation (NCEO) and the Centre for Environmental Data Analysis. It is supported by a worldwide community of stakeholders.
